There's no denying that 2020 has been an absolute dickhead. Lockdown, loss and uncertainty has had a devastating impact on the mental health of so many.

The Trussell Trust

Christmas is a time when food is important to everyone. However millions of people in communities up and down the country simply won't be able to afford the luxury that most of us are used to.

The Trussell Trust operate a network of foodbanks up and down the country to give food and support to support to those in crisis.
